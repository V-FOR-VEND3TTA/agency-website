Our Simple 3 Step Process

Some marketing agencies complicate stuff. We adhere to the Magical Rule of 3

Define

The first step is to define the target audience and goals of the marketing campaign. Who is the target audience? What are their needs, desires, and pain points? What is the desired outcome of the marketing campaign? This step is critical because it lays the foundation for the entire marketing strategy. It helps to ensure that the marketing efforts are focused on the right audience and that the desired outcome is clear and achievable.

Plan

Once the target audience and goals have been defined, the next step is to develop a marketing plan. The marketing plan should outline the tactics that will be used to reach the target audience and achieve the desired outcome. The plan should include a mix of tactics, such as social media, content marketing, email marketing, paid advertising, and more. The plan should also include a timeline, budget, and metrics for tracking the success of the campaign.

Execute & Optimize

This step involves implementing the tactics outlined in the marketing plan, such as creating and publishing content, running paid advertising campaigns, sending emails, and more. It also involves tracking the results of the campaign, such as website traffic, engagement, leads, and sales. Based on the results, the tactics can be adjusted and optimized to improve the overall effectiveness of the campaign.
